<p><strong>ACADEMIC POLICY AND FRAMEWORK</strong>
</p>
<p>Academic assessment is a process of gathering and documenting information
about students’ achievement and performance and is used for making informed
decisions about student, curricular and educational policies. It involves
giving and using feedback to enable students to improve their learning
and meet their academic goals.</p>
<p><strong>GRADING SYSTEM</strong>
</p>
<p>A good grasp of the grading system and promotion criteria helps you to
set your short-term and long-term goals, i.e. gaining the necessary standard
entry into a Junior College / Centralised Institute / Polytechnic / Institute
of Technical Education of your preferred choice. Goals must be achievable
and evaluated regularly.</p>
