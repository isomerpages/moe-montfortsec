<p>Dear Parents/Guardians</p>
<p>Welcome to the Montfortian family!</p>
<p>The Parent Support Group (PSG) is an integral part of Montfort Secondary
School. It is an active committee of dedicated and concerned parents, supporting
all school activities over the years that have helped enhance relations
and understanding between the school and parents as well as between parent
and child. It bridges the gap between the school, parent and the child.</p>
