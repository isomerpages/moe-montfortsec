<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>The IMDA Infocomm &amp; Multimedia Club is a platform for students to
explore and develop skills in information technology, digital media, and
creative design. Through hands-on experiences, members gain practical knowledge
in areas such as programming, multimedia production, and emerging technologies,
equipping them with skills relevant to the digital future.</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<p>• To nurture students’ interest in ICT, coding, and multimedia design.</p>
<p>• To provide opportunities for students to learn and apply skills in real-world
contexts.</p>
<p>• To develop problem-solving, teamwork, and leadership through tech-based
projects.</p>
<p>• To empower students to become responsible and discerning digital citizens.</p>
<h3>Key Programmes &amp; Activities</h3>
<h4>Infocomm Focus:</h4>
<p>• Coding and programming (e.g., Python, HTML/CSS, Scratch coding)</p>
<p>• Cybersecurity awareness and digital literacy workshops</p>
<p>• Participation in competitions like N.Emation, Our Schools Our Stories
and many more.</p>
<h4>Multimedia Focus:</h4>
<p>• Photography and videography</p>
<p>• Graphic design and photo editing (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom)</p>
<p>• Video production and editing for school events</p>
<p>• Audio and sound engineering for school performances</p>
<h4>Integrated Activities:</h4>
<p>• School event coverage (e.g., concerts, National Day, Speech Day)</p>
<p>• AI, AR/VR and emerging tech exploration sessions</p>
<h3>Skills Students Will Gain</h3>
<p>• Technical skills: coding, multimedia editing, digital content creation.</p>
<p>• Soft skills: teamwork, project management, creative problem-solving.</p>
<p>• Exposure to tech industry trends through workshops and learning journeys.</p>
<h4>CCA Schedule</h4>
<p>• Day/Time: Every Friday, 2.30pm to 5.30pm
<br>• Venue: Computer labs 1, 2 and 4</p>
